The rise of COVID-19 in New York State

The COVID-19 pandemic caused catastrophic effects for all. Communities were affected and had to act fast. COVID-19 tests were administered in order to track the number of cases, the positivity rate and more. New York State was one of the locations that monitored the data and the effects of COVID-19 in 2020 and 2021. New York state governor Andrew Cuomo said “The entire country has been playing catch up with this virus since day one, but in New York we have been trying to plan forward and get ahead of the problem.”

In illustration one, the bar chart showcases the months with the highest number of positive Covid cases in New York state in 2020. December led with the most cases at 990,711, showing an increase from March that had 193,551 cases.

In illustration two, the line graph showcases the day with the highest number of COVID-19 tests administered in New York State in December of 2020. December 17th had the most tests administered with 249,385 tests.
